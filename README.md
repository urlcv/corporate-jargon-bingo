5×5 bingo card of classic corporate jargon and workplace buzzwords. Play in your browser or print a card for your next meeting.

> **Live tool:** [urlcv.com/tools/corporate-jargon-bingo](https://urlcv.com/tools/corporate-jargon-bingo)
> This package powers the free Corporate Jargon Bingo tool on **[URLCV](https://urlcv.com)** — the recruitment platform that helps agencies present candidates professionally.

---

## Features

- **5×5 bingo grid** — 24 random jargon phrases + FREE centre square
- **Tone selector** — Mild (common meeting speak), Spicy (more eyeroll), or Mixed
- **Line detection** — Automatic detection of completed rows, columns, and diagonals
- **Full house** — Win by marking all 24 squares
- **Shareable seed** — `?seed=…` URL parameter reproduces the exact same card
- **Print / PDF** — Clean branded print layout optimised for A4 and Letter paper
- **Copy summary** — One-click share your result with a link back to the tool
- **100+ curated phrases** — Grouped by tone, all workplace-safe
- Frontend-only — runs entirely in the browser with Alpine.js, zero server load

---

## How it works

1. Open the tool or click **New card** to generate a random bingo card
2. Choose a tone preset: Mild, Mixed, or Spicy
3. Click/tap squares as you hear or read those phrases in meetings
4. The tool tracks completed lines and announces when you get a **Line!** or **Full house!**
5. Click **Print card** to print a clean branded card or save as PDF
6. Share a specific card layout via the `?seed=` URL parameter
